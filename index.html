Questions

Do I have to talk, or can I type?

Either. Tap and speak, or type your brain-dump — the AI handles both the same way.

How accurate is it?

It pulls dates, times, reminders, subtasks, and repeats straight from your own words, and gets it right most of the time. Everything it creates is fully editable, so you can tweak anything in a tap.

Is my data private?

Your tasks and memos are yours. You can export everything, or delete your account and all its data, at any time. See our privacy policy.

What does it cost?

Unpact is free to start, with a Pro upgrade that unlocks unlimited captures.

Which platforms?

iPhone first, with Android on the way.

Does it work offline?

Capturing needs a connection so the AI can transcribe and organize your dump. Your created tasks then live in your account.
